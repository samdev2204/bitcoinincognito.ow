Whitepaper

Bitcoin Incognito (XBI) is a cryptocurrency that takes Satoshi Nakamotos original vision for Bitcoin and adds to the concept in a way that makes it both more environmentally conscious and private for its users. Bitcoin Incognito being Proof-of-Stake with Masternodes means that users are able to mint coins without using any more energy than a personal computer. Masternodes also make for fast, secure transactions and are inherently resistant to 51% attacks. Bitcoin incognito uses zerocoin protocol to ensure that users will not be subjected to privacy invading analytics.

XBI COIN

Fair Launch

Bitcoin Incognito was distributed through a 1:1 XBI:BTC Airdrop on all exchanges who listed us followed by a Proof-of-Work phase for the first 5000 blocks.

Energy Saving

XBI uses a consensus mechanism to ensure the integrity of its BlockChain, instead of using an expensive proof of work algorithm to distribute the block reward to miners based on a winner per block, it uses Proof of Stake to reward the community for both staking coins and running Masternodes.

SwiftTX

Instant Transactions: SwiftTX transactions are confirmed and spendable within seconds, guaranteed by the network of masternodes, with no need to wait for multiple confirmations in order to be confident in the validity of the transaction.

Zerocoin

Zerocoin provides full privacy to the users of XBI. The implementation of the Zerocoin protocol converts publicly viewable XBI into anonymous zXBI. When a user wishes to spend zXBI, the XBI appear in the receivers wallet as regular XBI without history of where that XBI originated from providing real time incognito transactions.

Masternodes

Receive XBI rewards based on their availability and their ability to offer network services in a decentralized and trustless manner.

Open Source

Bitcoin Incognito's entire codebase is publicly available for viewing and verification by anyone. It will always remain open source and be maintained by an ever growing community.

Community Driven

In the nature of transparency and open source, Bitcoin Incognito is developed for the community and by the community.

Low Fees

Extremely low fees, with an average transaction fee of less than a few cents.

MASTERNODES

Owning and running a Bitcoin Incognito Masternode is a way that you can support the XBI network through mining.
Masternodes are commonly Virtual Private Servers (VPS) that store a copy of the Bitcoin Incognito blockchain. They support the networks decentralised nature and help to confirm transactions quickly.
As a result, masternodes receive rewards in the form of XBI for playing their part in supporting the network.

The XBI team is planning to launch a one-click masternode setup. Details will be announced soon!

FAQ

Q: How do I stake?

A: Please look in your wallet, and check if staking-arrow (lower right corner) is green. If not, then go to “/User/Appdata/XBI/xbi.conf” file and open it. It will probably be empty. Type : staking=1 and save the configuration file. Close wallet and reopen it again. Wait for it to be fully synched. Within approx. 1 minute the arrow will turn from grey to green.
Congratulate yourself and celebrate this event preferably with a beer or a wine (if 21+). You are staking so let the coins come to the master!

Q: How do I setup a masternode?

A: Follow the official guides. You can find them over our XBI discord group (under guides) or check below for the official guides.

Official XBI Masternode Guide: https://github.com/sub307/Bitcoin_Incognito/

Q: I am staking (the arrow down below at the right= green) . How long does it take before I get my staking-rewards?

A: This depends on how many coins you are staking and there is also a luckfactor involved. You can adjust your staking under coincontrol (send-tab in wallet) and under changing splitttreshold (see wallet under Tools- debug console, and type: ‘ help’)

Q: I set up my masternode. How long does it take before I get my first rewards?

A: You will recieve MN-rewards after approx. 48-60 hours after a successful setup. A successful setup can be checked over your SSH connection to VPS (for example Bitvise or Putty. Just type : xbi -cli masternode status.
If you get a “status 4” your masternode was installed correctly.
Congratulate yourself and celebrate this event preferably with a beer or a wine (if 21+). You will get your XBI Masternode rewards within the next 60 hours, so let the coins come to the master!

Q: How do I fix a Masternode that has stopped synching?

1. Login into the MN using Bitvise or your preferred SSH tool/terminal and type the following:
systemctl stop XBI.service

2. Then at the command line type:
xbid -reindex

3. Wait 15 minutes, if the reindex doesn’t report that it is finished, break out of it using:
Ctrl – C

(At this stage it may also help to add some MN addresses to the xbi.conf file on the MN)

4. To restart the XBI service type:
systemctl start XBI.service
